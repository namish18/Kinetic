What is a DID?

A Decentralized Identifier (DID) is a new type of identifier defined by the W3C. Unlike traditional usernames or emails, DIDs are:

  • Self-owned — You control your identity
  • Decentralized — No single point of failure
  • Verifiable — Cryptographically provable
  • Persistent — Stored on IPFS, not a server
